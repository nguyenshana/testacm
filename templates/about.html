ABOUT US

ACM SIGGRAPH Student Chapter at SJSU is a student organization at San Jos√© State University, in San Jose, CA. Formed out of a need to represent students with an interest in computer graphics, the club aims to be an asset to student life by connecting the SJSU community to the CG industry.

The ACM SIGGRAPH Student Chapter at SJSU focuses on three goals:

  1. Expanding awareness & presence of computer graphics technology on campus.
  2. Uniting and sparking collaboration between the design, digital art, engineering, and science departments.
  3. Connecting students to the companies, software, events, and technology of the computer graphics industry.
